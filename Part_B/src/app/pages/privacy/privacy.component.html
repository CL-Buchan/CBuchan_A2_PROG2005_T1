Security Considerations

Utilise strong passwords, implement multi-factor authentication where possible, use secure portals/websites when possible as most web services require a level of encryption to support and protect their users.

Privacy

Personal information or details will not be collected and stored. This principle does not apply to many organisations, where data and personal information is cruical for business revenue.

Some privacy practices for users are:

  • Do not provide sensitive information without reason and checks for legitimacy.
  • Do not provide more information than what is required.
  • Most 'professional' businesses will not ask for personal information post account creation. This may be a sign of a scammer.
